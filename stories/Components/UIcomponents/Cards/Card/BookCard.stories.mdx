# MANGROVE CARD (Book)

The Mangrove Book Card components are cards with information and/or image.

### Overview
The Mangrove Book Card component defines a card of information with some items. It is a multi-usage component that creates boxes that are usually teasing some kind of content.

#### When to use:

This is particularly suited for publications and brochures.

It is designed to have more on a row, perhaps 5 or 6 on desktop.

### Formatting

#### Default

The Book Card variant is more simplistic in content, with a smaller font size and no summary text or labels.

It's images is 3x4 in aspect ratio.
